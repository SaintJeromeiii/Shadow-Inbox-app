## Full description

Shadow Inbox turns email into an arcade run. Connect Gmail, and AI triage sorts noise from signal while you draft replies in your voice.

**What you get**

- **Auto-triage** — Messages scored by urgency and category (open cases, intel, noise).
- **Send-ready drafts** — Quick replies and tone-aware redrafts using your operator profile.
- **Executive briefing** — Daily crime-bulletin summary of what matters.
- **Arcade progression** — Clear mail to level up your fighter, hit daily goals, and keep streaks.
- **Privacy-first AI** — OpenAI runs on our server; your API keys never ship in the app.

**Requirements**

- Gmail account (Google sign-in)
- Internet connection

Privacy policy: https://shadow-inbox-production.up.railway.app/docs/privacy.html
